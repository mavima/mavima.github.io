Famtravel

Famtravel is an application for families travelling in European cities. It is a crowdsourcing platform for parents who want to share information and look for it. The app, based on my idea, was originally created at LeWagon coding bootcamp. We were a team of four students, I acted as the lead developer. Afterwards I continued working on the application and the first version was published in 2019.

Superpower rental app

This Rails MVC application was made at a LeWagon coding bootcamp during five days, together with three other students. It is practically a clone of the AirBnB site, we were just renting out superpowers rather than accomodation.

Cocktail recipe app

Another excercise site that I coded and designed. Search for a tasty drink or add your own favourite cocktail recipe.
