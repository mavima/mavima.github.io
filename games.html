Game Corner

❗ Sorry! As the other games require a keyboard, unfortunaly only Whack a Mole and Memory can be used with a mobile device.

Whack a mole - get those little rascals as fast as you can by clicking them with your mouse!

Tetris is an old classic - build complete lines of the cubes before they reach the top. Move the shapes with the keyboard

Help the little frog over the highway and the river. He jumps when you press the arrows on your keyboard.

Space Invaders is another retro game - no diplomatic skills are used here, just shoot the UFOs before they crush the Earth.

A simple memory card game. Click on the cards to form couples.

In this version of the old snake game the caterpillar is hungry and wants to eat apples. Move him with the keyboard.
